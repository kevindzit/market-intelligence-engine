standard income statement example:

Revenue - Money coming in from selling products/services
COGS (Cost of Goods Sold) - Direct costs to make/deliver the product
Gross Profit - Revenue minus COGS (profit before overhead)
R&D (Research & Development) - Money spent creating new products
SG&A (Selling, General & Administrative) - Salaries, marketing, rent, etc.
OpEx (Operating Expenses) - Total of R&D + SG&A
OpInc (Operating Income) - Gross Profit minus OpEx (actual profit/loss)
